Vertically Centered Layout

Rotated Horizontally Centered Layout with Spiky Leaves

This experiment implements improvements from the Usability Audit and a feedback interview with Prof. Dr. Eduard Glatz.

Instead of aligning all attributes, singly linked attributes (only occuring in one itemset) are not in the same list as the other attributes. They are positioned on the other side of the frequency node.

Rather than aligning the attributes horizontally they are now aligned vertically. These two improvements make the graph layout a lot more scalable. The algorithm is only a first version and thus not faultless. Nevertheless, it looks very promising and is being adapted for the Proof Of Concept.
