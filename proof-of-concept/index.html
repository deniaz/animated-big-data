Proof Of Concept

Animated Visualization of Frequent Itemsets as Hypergraphs

This Proof Of Concept is a consolidation of the results of the previous experiments. The layout algorithm is based on the Vertically Centered Layout experiment and the animation algorithm on the Animated Force Layout.

The library D3 was dropped in favour of a Custom Layout Algorithm and the SVG Library Snap.svg.
